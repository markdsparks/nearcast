Nearcast identity · round 3

We found it. Here's the system.

The app already converged on the answer: a luminous place-pin with a sun cresting a clean horizon — a sunrise over a place you care about. Round 3 turns that mark into a complete, shippable identity — app icon, glyph, favicon, and wordmark — so the old glossy app-store icon can finally retire.

Vision

Make weather feel as local and human as the moments people are planning around.

Mission

Nearcast turns live weather into clear, beautiful context for the places, plans, and hours people care about.

The mark in one line

A place you care about (the pin) and the sky over it at golden hour (sun rising over the horizon).
